About NFTs

What is NFT?, NFT is an abbreviation of Non-Fungible Token. NFTs are blockchain-based digital assets that are unique and cannot be exchanged for other assets. The growing popularity of these Tokens, has changed the way people view digital ownership, allowing people to own and trade them.
